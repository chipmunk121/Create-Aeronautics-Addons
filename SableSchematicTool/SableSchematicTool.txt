Name: Sable Schematic Tool
Description: Adds a blueprint save and load system for Sable sub-levels. Properly handles the complex state that sub-levels carry — block entities, entities, contraption anchors, rope connectors, and swivel bearings — remapping references correctly on placement rather than just copying raw data. Includes an in-game tool for saving and loading without typing commands.
