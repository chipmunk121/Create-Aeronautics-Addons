Name: Create Aeronautics Lift Patch
Description: Fixes the Sable lift and drag calculations to use a proper quadratic increase with velocity instead of the current linear calculation. Makes it easier to build both large and small planes in Create: Aeronautics since less wing area is needed to generate lift. Lift and drag coefficients can be adjusted in the config.
