Name: Aeronautic Additions and ChunkLoader
Description: An addon for Create: Aeronautics adding features felt to be missing from the base mod. Currently adds an Aeronautic Chunk Loader that, when placed on a Sable sub-level, keeps it and surrounding chunks loaded. Dynamically calculates the sub-level size and adjusts the loaded chunk area accordingly. Has no effect when placed outside a sub-level. Not recommended for very fast-moving aircraft.
