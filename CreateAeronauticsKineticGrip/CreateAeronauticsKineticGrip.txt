Name: Create Aeronautics: Kinetic Grip
Description: An addon for Create: Aeronautics that adds player-operable handles. Unlike the standard Aeronautics handles, these can be used directly from the player's point of view, and can be combined to craft hand-held tools such as chainsaws.
