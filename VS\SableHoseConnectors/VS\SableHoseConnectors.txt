Name: VS Hose Connectors
Description: Adds blocks that make ship-to-ship transportation in Valkyrien Skies simpler and more practical. Supports both fluid and item transport between ships, with experimental support for Create: Aeronautics.

- Hose Connector
- Magnet Hose Connector
- Item Hose Connector
- Item Magnet Hose Connector
- Electric Wire Connector
- Electric Magnet Wire Connector
- Chain Connector
