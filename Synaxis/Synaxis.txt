Name: Synaxis
Description: A comprehensive control and automation expansion for Sable. Build logic networks using comparators, boolean gates, weighted sums, vector utilities, flip-flops, and Lua-programmable units. Also adds configurable propulsion blocks including propellers, vector thrusters, and flaps that can be adapted to aircraft, ships, drones, or any custom mechanism.
