Name: Aeronautics Camera Sync
Description: A client-side mod that synchronizes the player's camera with the rotation of moving Create: Aeronautics contraptions. When standing on a ship or other dynamic structure, the view smoothly rotates along with it during turns, tilts, and aerial maneuvers instead of staying fixed to the world.
