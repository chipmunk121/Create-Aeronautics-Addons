Name: Copycats+ Aeronautics Weight
Description: A compatibility mod between Copycats+ and Create: Aeronautics that assigns accurate mass (kpg) values to all Copycats+ blocks for the Sable physics engine. Includes mass values for all Copycats+ block types, with variable blocks like copycat layers scaling their mass by the number of layers placed.
