Name: Auto Sable Physics
Description: An event-driven automation addon for Sable that watches for block changes, detects unsupported structures, and automatically converts them into Sable physics sub-levels. Break support blocks, cut trees, or collapse small unsupported structures and Sable handles the motion automatically. Physics objects can also auto-restore after being idle, and a Sable hammer item is included for manually pinning or restoring objects.
