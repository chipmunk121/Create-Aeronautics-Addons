Name: Create Propulsion: Simulated
Description: A port of Create: Propulsion to NeoForge 1.21.1 with full support for Sable and Create: Aeronautics. Adds thruster blocks that integrate directly with Sable contraption physics, including force-at-point behavior for proper torque and multi-thruster handling on assembled crafts.
Links:
- Modrinth | https://modrinth.com/mod/create-propulsion-simulated
- CurseForge | https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/create-propulsion-simulated
- Source | https://github.com/Propulsion-Team/create-propulsion-simulated
- Discord | https://discord.gg/JHcArsDhh4
Adds:
- Thruster (fuel-based propulsion)
- Ion Thruster (FE-powered propulsion)
- Creative Thruster (configurable high-power propulsion)
- Copycat Wings
