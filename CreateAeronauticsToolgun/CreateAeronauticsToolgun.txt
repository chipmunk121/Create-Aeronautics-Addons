Name: create aeronautics: toolgun
Description: A Create: Aeronautics expansion that brings VMod-style engineering tools to the vehicle environment. Supports saving, printing, deleting, and dragging physical structures including those with child substructures and container contents. Includes a blueprint system, a magnetic drag gun that limits applied force rather than hard-capping by mass, and welding mechanics.
